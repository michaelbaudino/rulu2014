CFP

Call For Proposal

You want to come and give a talk at RuLu? Please submit your talk!

How it works

Here is the CFP process in a few steps:

  1. you submit your talk(s) using the CFP form
  2. we may send emails to know more about your talk
  3. the CFP closes, we vote and select the talks
  4. eventually, we let you know if your talk has been selected

The CFP closes on March 31st, so we will give our definitive answer at the beginning of April.

About your talk

Your talk may or may not be about the Ruby language. But we suppose you take the point of view of a rubyist. Or you could do the opposite: talk to rubyists and propose a perspective change!

Don't forget to give a good abstract that reveals what is so important/unique/exciting about your talk!

We recommend you have a look at Mike Moore's blog post on How to write a conference proposal .

What you get

What you get, in a few words:

  • two nights at the speakers' hotel
  • a high quality video of your talk, published on Youtube
  • a diner with the other speakers, right before the conference
  • and a free ticket to the conference, of course

And we will help you as much as you can:

  • we give you feedbacks on your draft
  • we assist you so that your find your way to RuLu
  • we make everything to make you feel confortable

Travel expenses

We don't cover travel expenses but we encourage speaker sponsorship.

If a company covers your travel expenses, it becomes a speaker sponsor and get special thanks on stage and in the newsletter. And we make sure that the logo of the company is visible on the video of your talk.

Any question?

Please send an email to hello@rulu.eu if you've got any question.

Now it's about time to submit your talk! :)
