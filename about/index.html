About RuLu

So it's in France, right?

Ruby Lugdunum (aka Rulu) is a two-day conference dedicated to the Ruby Programming Language.
It happens every year in June, in Lyon, France.

RuLu features great talks,
plenty of time to meet people and awesome food!

RuLu 2014 will be the fourth edition of the
Ruby Lugdunum Conference.
